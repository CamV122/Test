About Us

We are a VRChat-based military group dedicated to realistic operations, disciplined structure, and strong community leadership. Our mission is to bring immersive military roleplay to VRChat with pride and respect.

Training Program

Our training includes Basic Training for recruits, Advanced Drills for NCOs, and Leadership Development for officers. Members must complete specific tasks to rank up.
